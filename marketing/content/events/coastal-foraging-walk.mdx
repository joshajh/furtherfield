Join us for a leisurely walk through Landguard Nature Reserve, learning to identify and sustainably harvest the edible plants that grow along our coastline.

## What We'll Cover

- Identification of common coastal edible plants
- Sustainable harvesting practices
- Basic preparation and cooking tips
- The cultural history of coastal foraging

## What to Bring

- Comfortable walking shoes
- Weather-appropriate clothing
- A small basket or bag for collecting
- Notebook and pen if you'd like to take notes

All materials and a light refreshment will be provided.
