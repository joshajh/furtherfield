Shore Line brings together five artists whose work explores the liminal space where land meets sea.

## Featured Artists

- **Maria Chen** - Large-scale photographs of erosion patterns
- **Tom Richards** - Sculptures made from beach-found materials
- **Sarah Ahmed** - Sound installation capturing tidal rhythms
- **James O'Connor** - Paintings of coastal weather systems
- **Yuki Tanaka** - Video work documenting shoreline changes

## Exhibition Dates

The exhibition runs from 1st March to 30th April 2025, open daily from 10am to 5pm.

Free entry. Donations welcome.

## Gallery Talks

Join us for artist talks every Saturday at 2pm throughout the exhibition run.
